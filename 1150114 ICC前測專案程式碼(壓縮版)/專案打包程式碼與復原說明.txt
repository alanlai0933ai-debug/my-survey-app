專案打包程式碼

# 1. 清理垃圾檔案 (刪除 node_modules, dist, .netlify, .firebase)
Write-Host "正在清理專案 (瘦身中)..." -ForegroundColor Cyan
Remove-Item -Path "node_modules", "dist", ".netlify", ".firebase", "functions/node_modules" -Recurse -Force -ErrorAction SilentlyContinue

# 2. 執行壓縮 (打包成 ZIP)
$zipName = "Project_Backup_" + (Get-Date -Format 'yyyyMMdd_HHmm') + ".zip"
Write-Host "正在打包成 $zipName ..." -ForegroundColor Cyan
Compress-Archive -Path * -DestinationPath $zipName -Force -ErrorAction SilentlyContinue

# 3. 完成提示
Write-Host "✅ 打包完成！檔案名稱：$zipName" -ForegroundColor Green


⚡ 未來如何復活？ (到了新電腦)
